Curve Geometry Window
=====================

The Curve Geometry window shows angles, distances, and areas for the currently
active curve (displayed in the Digitize toolbar). Regions in the window may be
selected and exported to other applications by copying (Ctrl+C) or dragging.

Measurements
------------

**Angle**
    At each point, the angle between the line from the previous point and the
    line to the next point.

**Distance**
    At each point, the length of the line to the next point.

**Functional area (FuncArea)**
    The area calculated by integrating the area under the curve.

**Polygonal area (PolyArea)**
    The area within the polygon formed by all points in the curve.

Until the coordinates are defined by axes points or a scale bar, measurements
are shown in screen coordinates (pixels). Afterwards they are in curve
coordinates.
