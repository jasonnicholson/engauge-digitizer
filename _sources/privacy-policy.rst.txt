Privacy Policy
==============

Engauge Digitizer does not collect any personal information.

The application runs entirely on your computer. It does not transmit data,
analytics, or usage information to any server.

**GitHub Issues**

If you file an issue on the
`GitHub issue tracker <https://github.com/jasonnicholson/engauge-digitizer/issues>`_,
any information you provide (description, screenshots, data files) is used
solely to troubleshoot and resolve your issue. We do not share this information
for any other purpose.
