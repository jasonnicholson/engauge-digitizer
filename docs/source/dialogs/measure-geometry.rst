Measure Geometry Window
=======================

The Measure Geometry window shows angles, distances, and areas for the currently
active measure (displayed in the Digitize toolbar). Regions in the window may
be selected and exported to other applications by copying (Ctrl+C) or dragging.

Measurements
------------

**Angle**
    At each point, the angle between the line from the previous point and the
    line to the next point.

**Distance**
    At each point, the length of the line to the next point.

**Functional area (FuncArea)**
    Not useful for measures; may be ignored.

**Polygonal area (PolyArea)**
    The area within the polygon formed by all points in the measure.

Until the coordinates are defined by axes points or a scale bar, measurements
are shown in screen coordinates (pixels). Afterwards they are in measure
coordinates.
