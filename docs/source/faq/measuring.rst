Measuring Angles, Distances, and Areas
=======================================

The :doc:`../dialogs/curve-geometry` and :doc:`../dialogs/measure-geometry`
windows show various measurements for the currently active curve and measure,
respectively.

The geometry windows are hidden by default:

- To show the **Curve Geometry** window: **View > Curve Geometry Info**
- To show the **Measure Geometry** window: **View > Measure Geometry Info**
